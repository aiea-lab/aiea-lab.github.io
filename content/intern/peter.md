Peter is a high school researcher focused on mechanistic interpretability and AI alignment. He is a co-founder and principal investigator at Substrate Labs, an independent non-profit AI research lab studying alignment, interpretability, world models, and the societal impact of frontier AI systems. He is also an affiliated researcher at MIT Critical Data, where he works on machine learning methods for high-stakes inference and structured probabilistic modeling. His current projects include sparse-feature analysis in domain-specialized language models, coherence-grounded calibration of language and vision models, and mechanistic reverse-engineering of frontier world models.
